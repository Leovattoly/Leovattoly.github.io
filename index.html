About me

I am an AI researcher and Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions Fellow at the Basque Center for Applied Mathematics (BCAM) in Bilbao, Spain, and a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the University of the Basque Country (UPV/EHU), working within the EU Horizon Europe project CINEMA.

My research applies deep learning to polymer reaction engineering: reverse-engineering the reaction conditions needed to reach target particle morphologies in emulsion polymerization, and detecting process deviations early — while a reaction is still running — using early time series classification.
